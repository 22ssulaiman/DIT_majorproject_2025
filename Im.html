TONY STARK

A genius billionaire and inventor who relies on his highly advanced suit of armor to fight alongside the Avengers. His suit grants him superhuman strength, durability, flight, and an array of weapons, including repulsor blasts and missiles. Stark is also a brilliant engineer and tech genius, constantly upgrading his suit with new features and capabilities. As a former weapons manufacturer turned hero, his character arc revolves around redemption and using his intelligence and resources for good.
